\begin{abstract}
We present a cosmological model derived from the Anti-Entropic Principle that requires zero free parameters. The theory predicts $H_0 = 73.63$ km/s/Mpc and $S_8 = 0.758$, resolving major cosmological tensions while maintaining excellent fit to CMB data.
\end{abstract}
